Biography

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of ECE at University of Alberta, advised by Dr. Di Niu. I am funded by AIGSS and Dept. of ECE, University of Alberta. My primary research interest is distributed machine learning, especially distributed optimization for large-scale machine learning applications.

I obtained my master’s degree from Xidian University, advised by Prof. Ning Cai, and bachelor’s degree also from Xidian University.
